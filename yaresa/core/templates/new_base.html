with just few taps

JED exists to take absolute responsibilities of the healthcare of individuals who due to their busy schedules or occupation find it difficult to spend time at health facilities. Our clients don’t have to worry at all since JED does a complete management of their health. Periodic medical screenings or check-ups will be done for them at their convenience. For clients who are on chronic medications and scheduled for appointments at various facilities, JED will set up reminders and notifications to pre-empt them when to follow up on their appointments as well as when to go for their refill of medications.
JED believes that just as individuals have personal lawyers, mechanics, pastors, housekeepers etc. individuals need an organisation that also manages every aspect of their health which will be available to them 24/7.

The Yaresa mobile application is here to help you book medical appointments with our first class medical professionals and have access to your secured medical information and records anywhere in the world.Carry your medical history with you anywhere you go. Having your medical history always available means in case of an emergency anywhere you are in the world,a large part of your problem have been solved just by looking through your medical history. If a medical officer knows your medical history, your condition is already under control.

Personal Health Details

Have access to your personal health details such as blood type, blood sugar level history, sickle cell status, G6PD deficiency status etc. with just some few taps. Get all these information always available for you everywhere you go.

Doctor Visits

Make consultation appointments or bookings with the yaresa mobile application by choosing your own date and time as to when you want to see a specialist or doctor.

Medication

Get the type of medication or drugs to take sent to you on the yaresa mobile application. Which means with the application, doctors or specialists can make drug prescription for you even at the comfort of your home.

Directions

Do not know where to find the hospital or laboratory to take your tests? Get the directions to any of our registered hospitals or laboratories using the yaresa mobile application maps. Never get stranded. Use the app to find any hospital or lab.

Diagnosis

Easy access to both your past and present diagnostic results is key to getting quick and effective medical care. Get your diagnostic records with just a few taps. Avoid having to always start from scratch because you cannot find your diagnosis records and imaging scans.

Notifications

Get notified by the yaresa app on the time and date to see our specialists, the time to get your laboratory tests, time to get your medication refills. The app also notifies you when it is time to take your drugs so you that you get the maximum benefit from your medications.

Personal Health Management

We at JED believe that your health is the key to a better and peaceful life. We take charge of managing your health by providing you with medical specialists, laboratory tests and diagnosis, medications or drugs and more. We also run an online laboratory and diagnosis service meaning you can access your laboratory test results anytime and anywhere you are. We provide you with the best and stress-free hospital placements only to be attended by our specialist doctors.


Medical Certification and Consultation

We provide medical examination and certification for people who are travelling and need a medical certificate to get a visa, for people whose job applications require a medical certificate etc. .We also give pre-wedding counselling and screening to couples who plan on getting married. All these certificates are given after conducting all the neccessary laboratory tests and diagnosis. By booking an appointment using the Yaresa app, we provide you with medical consultation, health education and counselling.


General Services

Our general services include ambulance and emergency services. Contact us via the yaresa app for emergency cases. We provide homecare for members who cannot go to the hospital. Blood and urine samples required for tests can be taken even at your own house. We also provide medical equipments and supplies. We have a locum placement agency that provide top medical professionals as stand in medical officers.
